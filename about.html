The Oldest Work in My Repetoire : Me!
Graduate Student / College Professor

Hello! :D

I am a graduate student currently pursuing my MFA in Digital Production Arts at Clemson University. I earned a BFA in Digital Media from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, where I now also teach students how to code in the Interactive Web Design course ( Fun Fact : I also built this website from scratch! ). Animation and Film have always been of great interest to me, but never seemed like a career I could achieve; until one day after watching Disney's Treasure Planet I had the realization But I Can, and so I Did. This mindset still drives me forward as I pursue my masters. I have learned so much at Clemson, mostly through trial and error, and despite roadblocks and failed attemtps, I always know I can and I will. One encounter I had with our department director sums up my experience really well; he asked me "Are you survivng?" to which I responded "No. I'm thriving."
