3D Modeling
I have experience in all facets of the 3D pipeline from modeling, script writing, rigging, and animating to surfacing, texturing, and rendering. I have also used various software that allows for more polished work outside of the native Maya package. Some examples include RenderMan, Substance Designer and Painter, Unreal Engine 4, and Nuke.

Bathroom Scene
For this piece I wanted to recreate realistic lighting through a stained glass bathroom window
I modeled everything in the scene from scratch and textured them using Arnold surfaces
The window took the longest, as I had to recreate the interior metal work with curves and extrude cylinders along them
I used about 7 lights to fully flesh out the 'sun' and it's highlights
