Child's Play
Child's Play is a passion project of mine that I have had the priviledge of working on with another student at Clemson. As the production lead and storyboard artist I conceptualized and storyboarded the entire film before any other work began. After the inital ground work had been laid, I took the role as lead animator and layout artist.

In the final section of film I animated shots :
4 . 5 . 6 . 7 . 11 . 16 . 17 . 19 . 21 . 22 . 23

One of the most fun challenges this project brought was figuring out how to express emotion through posing characters who had no faces and traditional bodies. In the boarding process, I focused on pushing the characters posing through their "spines" to help portray their emotions.
